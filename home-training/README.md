# Home Training

Recordable ball mastery content for KP13 Akademi players and parents.

## Contents

### Full Session (`full-session.md`)
A complete 35-minute ball mastery session. Suitable for individual training or recording as a reference video for clients.

- Warm-up (5 min)
- Foundation ball mastery (8 min)
- Advanced combinations (10 min)
- Game-realistic scenarios (8 min)
- Cool down (4 min)

**Equipment:** 1 ball, 8-10 cones, 10x10m space

### Recording Guide (`recording-guide.md`)
How to film the full session professionally — camera angles, setup, editing tips.

### Short Sessions (`short-sessions/`)
10-15 minute sessions designed to be sent to U9 players and their parents to do between team trainings. Each session:
- One focused skill theme
- 3-4 exercises with simple language (no coaching jargon)
- Minimal equipment: just a ball and a small space
- Easier/harder variants included

| Session | Theme |
|---------|-------|
| [session-01.md](short-sessions/session-01.md) | First touch |
| [session-02.md](short-sessions/session-02.md) | Turns |
| [session-03.md](short-sessions/session-03.md) | Weak foot |
| [session-04.md](short-sessions/session-04.md) | Ball familiarity & coordination |
| [session-05.md](short-sessions/session-05.md) | Speed & quick feet |

## Philosophy

- Technical mastery before speed — perfect slowly, then add pace
- Both feet — equal work, or more on the weaker foot
- High repetition — quality touches build mastery
- Keep it fun — especially for younger players
