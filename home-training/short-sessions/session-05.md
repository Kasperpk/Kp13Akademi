# Home Session 05 — Speed & Quick Feet

**Time:** 12-15 minutes  
**Equipment:** 1 ball, 3 cones or water bottles  
**Space:** 6-8 steps of open space  

Fast feet with the ball is what separates good dribblers from great ones. This session trains your feet to move quickly and your brain to react fast. Work hard — these exercises are meant to be challenging!

---

## 1. Warm-Up — 2 minutes

Jog lightly back and forth in your space. Then do 10 quick toe taps on the ball to wake up your feet.

---

## 2. Fast Toe Taps — 3 minutes

Tap the top of the ball as fast as you can, alternating feet.

**How to do it:**
- 20 seconds fast taps, 10 seconds rest
- 20 seconds fast taps, 10 seconds rest
- 20 seconds fast taps, 10 seconds rest
- 20 seconds maximum speed, 10 seconds rest

**Tip:** Stay light on your feet — don't stomp. Small, quick taps.

**Easier:** Go at a comfortable pace and build up speed over the sets.  
**Harder:** Count your taps each set and try to beat your score.

---

## 3. Slalom Dribble — 5 minutes

Set up 3 cones in a line, about 1-2 steps apart. Dribble through them as fast as you can.

**How to do it:**
- Go through the cones and back — that's 1 run
- Do 6 runs, rest 20 seconds
- Do 6 more runs, rest 20 seconds

**Tip:** Use the inside and outside of your foot to weave between cones. Keep the ball close — don't kick it too far ahead.

**Easier:** Space the cones further apart.  
**Harder:** Try to go faster each run, or use only your weak foot.

---

## 4. 1-2-3 Touch Sprint — 3 minutes

Take 3 quick touches to push the ball forward, sprint after it, stop it, and come back.

**How to do it:**
- Touch 1: push ball slightly forward with inside right foot
- Touch 2: push further with inside left foot
- Touch 3: push forward harder — now sprint to catch it
- Stop it with your sole
- Dribble back and repeat
- Do 8 runs

**Tip:** The sprint comes after touch 3. Push the ball into space and chase it — like in a real game.

---

## 5. Cool Down — 1-2 minutes

Walk slowly and tap the ball softly between feet. Shake out your legs.

---

## Well done! Speed with the ball gets better every session — keep it up!
