## 🧠 Key Messages for the Players (Pre-Tournament Talk)
1. **"Move to help your teammates"** — Don't stare at the ball. When you don't have it, move to create space
2. **"Think before the ball arrives"** — Scan, know your next move before you receive
3. **"Make the simple play"** — Short, quick passes. Don't hold on too long
4. **"Don't hold on to the ball too long"** — 1 or 2 touches where possible to keep the game flowing
5. **"Enjoy it"** — Learn from today and have fun

## 💬 Between Games Coaching Points

Keep it short and positive. Pick **1–2 points max** per break:

- ✅ *"When you don't have the ball — MOVE. Create space for your teammate"*
- ✅ *"Scan before the ball comes to you — know your next move already"*
- ✅ *"First touch with purpose — where do you want to go next?"*
- ✅ *"Quick passes keep the game flowing — don't hold it too long"*
- ✅ *"You're doing great — enjoy it!"*
