# U9 Team Session (La Masia) — 2026-05-13

**Session Theme (Red Thread):** Spatial awareness and being able to move the ball on inside the team

**Context:** team  
**Date:** 2026-05-13  
**Players:** 10
**Total Duration:** 90 minutes (including transitions)  

---

## The session purpose

The purpose of this session is to help the kids becoming better at spatial awareness, something they will need when they go up against the good teams in the tournaments coming up. 

We need to see
- Hard work
- Intensity
- Willingness to play
- Intelligence with and without the ball

So today we work on the aspects of the game that are mostly about movement and spatial awareness. It's important that players can think for themselves on the pitch and make some decisions in terms of their positioning and do thinks fast. See how Barcelona players move around, create space for eachother, look forward, make good first touches, turn up their body positioning. Make quick and good first touches. 

So the rough plan for this training is. 

- Short warmup with footwork, passsing and technical drills.

**Footwork**
Queues of 3-4 people

* 2 steps in, 2 steps sideways, back and forth, in and out, cross over, square move. 

**Footwork with passing plus technical drill**
** One man goes two-step in, runs up, recieve pass, back-peddles, recieve pass, pass back, turns up, recieves on back foot, pass down to other man, he dribbles through cones up to the next. 

(the third man has gone down to the other cone), he beings two-step in, run up recieve pass, back peddles, pass back, recieve pass, pass, back turns around and recieves on back foot, pass down to other man. He now dribbles through cones and up. 
When he is finised the first man goes up.
