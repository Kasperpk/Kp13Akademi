## Sprint Priming & Warm-Up — 8 min

Get the body ready for explosive work before any sprinting. Each ~10m line and back.

- **High knees** — runners warmup
- **Heel kicks** — flick heels to glutes, stay on toes, 2x down-and-back
- **Single leg hops** — 5 hops on right, 5 on left, soft landing
- **Broad jumps** — 5 two-footed forward, stick landing
- **Move goal** — 5 times pushing a goal

> Coaching cue: arms drive the sprint — make sure Emil pumps his arms properly from the start.
