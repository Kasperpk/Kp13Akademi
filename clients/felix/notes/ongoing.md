## 2026-04-14 — Initial assessment

Felix is technically very clean. Great passing foot and always picks the right pass. The key development area is dynamism — he defaults to the safe pass even when driving forward is the better option. He needs to develop the physical ability to carry the ball at speed AND the decision-making to recognise when to drive vs. pass. Secondary focus is his movement off the ball — how to get free of markers as a central player, checking shoulders, creating angles. Intensity is always threaded into every session.
