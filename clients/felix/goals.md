## Technical Areas to Improve

- Driving the ball at speed (push-and-go technique, longer touches into space)
- Movement off the ball to create receiving angles and get free of markers
- Decision-making: when to pass vs. when to drive
- Receiving under pressure on the half-turn

## Strengths to Build On

- Excellent passing foot — weight and accuracy of pass
- Technique and comfort on the ball
- Unselfishness — plays the simple and correct pass consistently
- Good positional sense as a central player
