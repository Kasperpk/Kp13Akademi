# History — Felix

Narrative milestones. Append-only. Write an entry every **6–8 weeks**, and after every tournament.

This is the story a graph can't tell. EPM scores will move up and down, benchmark numbers will tick over — this file says **what that actually looked like on the pitch**, and ties it back to the evidence (session dates, tournament matches, parent observations).

**Three things each entry should answer:**
1. Where is Felix now that he wasn't before?
2. What's the evidence? (session dates, benchmark deltas, tournament moments, parent quotes)
3. What does this unlock next?

---

## 2026-04-14 — Starting point

**Where Felix is:** 8-year-old central player with excellent technique and a great passing foot. Unselfish decision-maker who consistently picks the simple, correct pass. When he receives wide or finds space centrally, he cannot drive the ball at pace — defenders catch him because he steps on the ball rather than carrying it forward.

**Observable anchors (starting point):**
- Clean passing under low-to-medium pressure
- Comfortable on the ball in rondos
- Stalls when space opens in front of him

**What the program is working on next:** see [goals.md](goals.md) — driving the ball with speed, game-brain movement off the ball, intensity in play.

**Evidence source:** initial observation notes, session [2026-04-14](sessions/2026-04-14.md).
