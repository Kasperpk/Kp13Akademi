# Training Sessions

This directory contains training sessions for individual football coaching for clients aged 4-25.

## Structure

The sessions are organized into two main programs:
- **proforløb1/** - Pro Course 1 sessions
- **proforløb2/** - Pro Course 2 sessions (advanced level)

### Additional Standalone Sessions:
- **indoor-training-age10.md** - 40-minute comprehensive indoor training session for 10-year-old aspiring top player (winter conditions)
- **indoor-training-age10-quick-reference.md** - Quick reference guide for recording the indoor training session
- **strength-training-fundamental.md** - 30-minute fundamental strength training for professional football preparation (ages 16-25)

### Key Components

Each session typically includes:

1. **Opvarmning (Warm-up)** - 5-10 minutes
   - Increase body temperature
   - Mobilize joints
   - Prepare the body

2. **Atletiske bevægelser & hurtighed (Athletic movements & speed)** - 10-30 minutes
   - Improve acceleration
   - Change of direction
   - General movement quality

3. **Ball Mastery/Teknik (Ball Control/Technique)** - 10-25 minutes
   - Create confidence and control with the ball
   - Dribbling exercises
   - Ball control

4. **Afleveringer og indgå i spil (Passing and play integration)** - 10-30 minutes
   - Train engaging in play with teammates
   - Few touches with speed

5. **Afslutninger (Finishing)** - 10-20 minutes
   - Train finishing on goal
   - Various angles and situations

### Exercise Notation

- **Duration:** Typically written as "3x1 minut" (3 sets of 1 minute) or "3x40 sek" (3 sets of 40 seconds)
- **Repetitions:** Written as "10x" (10 repetitions)
- **Notes:** Coaching notes and observations are included in italics

## Language

Sessions are primarily written in Danish, with Session 10 in English. The terminology used:

- **Opvarmning** = Warm-up
- **Formål** = Purpose
- **Øvelser** = Exercises
- **Afslutninger** = Finishing
- **Afleveringer** = Passing
- **Dribling** = Dribbling

## Proforløb Levels

- **Proforløb 1** - Foundation level training sessions
- **Proforløb 2** - Advanced level with focus on integrating good training habits including self-training, calendar management, goal setting, and advanced agility
