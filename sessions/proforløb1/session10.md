# Session 10

## Overview
Work on speed, turns, quick feet are all qualities that are crucially important for football players.

## 1. Warm-up – 5 minutes
**Purpose:** Increase body temperature, mobilize joints and prepare the body.

## 2. Agility Stations – 20-25 minutes
**Purpose:** Improve acceleration, change of direction and general movement quality.

### First Station:
- 3 x straight stationary sprints (20-30 seconds, small rest in between)
- 3 x side sprints
- 3 x straight stationary sprints (20-30 seconds with forward leap between every four steps)

### Second Station:
- 3x Side jumps (20-30 seconds)
- 3x One leg side jumps (20-30 seconds)
- 3x turns with forward sprint

**5-10 minute break:** Talk about the last game and what he can work on. Show a video of what we are going to do next.

## 3. Master the Ball – 20-25 minutes
**Purpose:** Make creative and productive use of the ball.

### Master ball between two cones:
- 6x 20-30 seconds of high intensity side to side dribbling (each 2-3 with weak foot)
- 6x quick turns with the ball between two cones, end with sprint with ball
- 10x cone weave single foot strong foot, finish with a couple with weak foot
- 10x cone weave with angles

**5-10 minute break:** Talk about last game and what he can work on. Show video of what we going to do next.

## 4. Mechanical Exercises – 15 minutes
**Purpose:** Improve movement quality and ball control.

**Exercises:**
- 5-10x Run up make step over ball and stop like you would shoot or turn
- 5-10x Run up to ball and make quick turn in some direction
- 3x dribbling figure of 8ths with cone call

## 5. Passing – 10 minutes
**Purpose:** Train passing with intensity.

**Exercises:**
- Intensity passing 3x1 minutes

## 6. Master Finishing – 15-20 minutes
**Purpose:** Train finishing on goal.

**Exercises:**
- 10x Side to side dribble contact between 4-8 contacts before shooting (do a couple with weak foot)
- 10x cone weave and finishing
- 10x inside outside drag between cones
- 10x inside outside drag and shoot
